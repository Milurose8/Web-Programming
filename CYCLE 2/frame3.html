Red Fort

The Red Fort in Delhi was built in the mid 17th century by the famous Mughal emperor Shah Jahan. It is
located in Old Delhi and this architectural genius was home to many Mughal emperors for a long time.
The fort is segregated into various sections that include entertainment halls, baths, canals, balconies,
mosques, etc and it boasts of Indo-Islamic and Mughal designs. This massive fort is 75-feet high and has
been declared a world heritage site by UNESCO. Every year, the prime minister of our country unfurls our
national flag from Red Fort on Independence Day.
